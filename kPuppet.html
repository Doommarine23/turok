uint& PlayerFlags()

bit field of player state flags:

  • PF_NOCLIP – noclip is enabled
  • PF_FLY – fly mode is enabled
  • PF_DEAD
  • PF_GOD – god mode or invincibility is enabled
    (spiritual invincibility does not affect this flag)
  • PF_JUMPING – it's the first few frames of a jump, and the jump button is still pressed
  • PF_HASJUMPED – in the air from jumping (not from walking off a ledge)
  • PF_INWARPAREA
  • PF_CLIMBTHRUST – player is on a climbable surface and holding forward
    (true even during pause between actual motions)
  • PF_NOAIRFRICTION
  • PF_CRAWLING – in a crawl space, like the small passages in the catacombs
  • PF_PREVENTDEATHCAM
  • PF_FIREDPROJECTILE
